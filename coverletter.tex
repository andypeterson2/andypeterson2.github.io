\begin{cvletter}

\lettersection{About Me}
I am a software engineer with experience building quantum computing platforms, cloud infrastructure, and full-stack applications. I co-founded the Quantum Computing at UCSD student organization and led software engineering at Qualcomm Institute, developing quantum-encrypted video systems and QML classification models.

\lettersection{Why Me?}
My background spans quantum computing research, full-stack DevOps engineering, and technical team leadership. I bring deep expertise in Python, distributed systems, and emerging technologies, paired with the communication skills to translate complex systems into clear, impactful solutions.

\end{cvletter}

tech-debt.register — CV Editor

Engineering · Debt Register · Round Two

Round one, cleared. Round two, open.

Round one is done — 13 of 14 items resolved and shipped to main, CI-green, one deferred on an upstream Vite 8 bug. Then a second wave landed: the demo reframe, an interruptible guided tour, a real menubar, and undo across the whole editor — now spanning profile switches. Seven commits, and a fresh audit surfaced seven new items, scored and ranked below — and separately, removing macro support reopened an eighth (item 03, whose whole verdict rested on those macros), already fixed. Five are now shipped, one was built then reassessed away (the dedup — persist() had already closed its risk), and two remain — the cheapest guards, still cheap. The headline signal: the store the round-one split trimmed to 587 lines ballooned to 969 as undo and the per-profile cache landed — then item 20's extraction clawed it back to 933.

Scope: the CV editor (Svelte island) and the surfaces it touches — the cv Express backend, the site's deploy/headers, and the test suite.  Not covered: the other portal apps (classifiers, nonogram) and the gateway, which have their own registers.  Status as of 2026-07-08.

Code · Correctness ✓ Resolved

Item 03 was closed as working-as-intended: tex() left \ { } ~ ^ unescaped so fields could carry the real CV's custom macros. That support was removed, voiding the rationale — a literal brace or backslash now just breaks the compile, with nothing left to protect.

55d52cb · supersedes item 03 Shipped: a permitted-symbol allowlist (symbols.ts, 78 commands) — tex() substitutes \rightarrow→→, \alpha→α to their Unicode glyphs (xelatex is Unicode-native), then escapes every remaining special.
